Blended Academy is an online private tutoring platform that promotes knowledge sharing by connecting students with tutors, teachers and mentors to facilitate online learning.

Our mission is to provide a convenient one-stop learning platform that facilitates knowledge sharing and skills development

At Blended Academy, our actions are guided by the following values: Ethical Conduct; Excellence; Innovation; Diversity; Client-Centric. We are committed to providing a healthy and conducive virtual working environment where students can learn and have fun with their matched tutors..

This platform provides services predominately in academic tutoring offering the following subjects: Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Mathematical Literacy and Business Studies. However, our upcoming platform will also extend to other areas such as Music, Cooking, Photograph, Art, Sports, Health and Wellbeing, and Languages.

After completing an online application (see links above) which takes an average of three minutes, blended member will be in contact with you and connect you with your potential tutor. You can flexibly discuss your schedule with your tutor and further make arrangements for your sessions (subject to change in our new platform).

Make sure you have a laptop, or a mobile device with a stand that can hold your mobile firmly during the session. In addition, ensure that you have a good connection and a healthy environment where there may not be disturbances. Your sessions are normally scheduled by you and your tutor. Commonly used platform are: Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Team as our online tool. FYI. you may need to download one of this platforms on your device, alternatively, you may use web browser (subject to change in our new platform).
